Salon Website

The project

Completed January 2020. This a static website for a Salon in Ohio. This company has been in business since 2011 and did not have a website. The business’s intention for was website wasn’t necessary to gain new cliental, but they wanted to have an online presence and be able to provide basic information for clients that are not on social media. The website contains all store and personal information as well as a photo gallery, services menu and contact form. The contact email form is currently down due to client request. This project used a bootstrap theme, node.js and express.

Focus

My focus on this project was to walk through a whole life cycle of a freelance project. I met with the client to scope out the project, understand their needs and wants, and provide a great service and product to the client. I setup everything from purchasing the domain, wireframing, designing and then hosting the website. We also set up a photoshoot with professional photographer so they could have quality images on their site.

What I learned

I learned how to set up email forms and I am becoming more comfortable with hosting on Heroku. I big take a way from this project was learning about client management. I learned the importance of setting up clear expectations and deliverables with timelines that fall on both the client and the developer to fulfill.
